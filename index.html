About

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Previously, I received B.Eng degrees in Computer Science from School of Information, Renmin University in 2021.

My research interests lie in the area of information retrieval. Specifically, I focus on solving the query ambiguity problem through search result diversification and personalized search in both web search and product search domains. Recently, I am also interested in the crossing field between information retrieval and large language models.
